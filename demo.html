What Is Light Demolition?

We Handle the Jobs You've Been Putting Off

Light demolition means we take down structures that don't require heavy equipment or engineering permits — decks, sheds, fences, hot tubs, swing sets, small outbuildings. These are the projects that people put off for years because they don't know who to call.

You call us. We show up with the right tools, tear it down safely, sort and load every piece, and haul it all away. When we leave, the only thing left is a clean, open space.

We serve homeowners and contractors across Coeur d'Alene, Spokane, Post Falls, Hayden, Rathdrum, Spokane Valley, and all surrounding communities — with the same flat-rate pricing and owner-operated reliability on every job.
