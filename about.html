Who We Are

Born and Raised in Coeur d'Alene

We're Frank and Romen — two brothers who grew up right here in North Idaho, raised by parents who taught us that the way you do anything is the way you do everything.

Growing up in CDA, we spent our time outdoors — hiking the trails around Lake Coeur d'Alene, fishing, and connecting with the people and places that make this area special. This valley isn't just where we work. It's home. And we take that seriously.

We started Simple Junk Removal because we kept hearing the same frustration from people in our community: you hire a junk removal company and either nobody shows up, the price doubles at the end, or some random crew shows up at your house and you have no idea who they are. That bothered us. So we built something different.

When you call Simple Junk Removal, an owner answers. When we show up to your job, an owner is there. You'll see the same faces that were on the other end of the phone. Every time. No exceptions.

We've done over 100 jobs — for homeowners, realtors, and contractors across Coeur d'Alene, Spokane, Post Falls, and every community in between. Every single one has rated us five stars. That's not something we take lightly. That's the standard we hold ourselves to on every job we do.
